Purpose of Site

This site provides a comprehesive list of advice to help beginner grows get into the hobby of growing various species of carnivorous plants.

Basic information is presented with the hopes of helping those who might be getting started in growing.

Information is based on real life experiences of growing these plants in both and indoor and outdoor setting on Vancouver Island, hardiness zone 9a.

Average temperatures in my region can be expected to range between 15°C-23°C in the summer and range between 1°C-7°C in winter. Highs can reach 37°C and lows can get down to -18°C.

Key Concepts

  • Provide basic plant information
  • Give a comprehesive guide on plant care
  • Offer real world tips and tricks learned from experience
  • Create a repository gallery of continued documentation of personal plants
  • Cultivate a friendly atmosphere where questions can be answered (WIP)
